About the Project

This platform aims to improve the interaction between citizens and their elected representatives. By providing an easy way for citizens to report issues, provide feedback, and receive updates, the platform enhances transparency and responsiveness in governance.

Roles in the Platform:

  • Admin: Oversee platform operations, manage user roles, and ensure data integrity.
  • Citizen: Report issues, provide feedback, and receive updates from politicians.
  • Politician: Respond to citizen concerns, post updates, and engage in discussions.
  • Moderator: Monitor interactions, ensure respectful communication, and resolve conflicts.
